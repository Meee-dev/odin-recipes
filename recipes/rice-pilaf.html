Delicious and tender pilaf rice

This pilaf rice is an absolute delight and tender, flavorful, and so good you can enjoy it all on its own. It also pairs beautifully with meat dishes or rich sauces.

Ingredients for 4 people

  • 50 cl vegetables or beef broth
  • 1 oignon
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • 250 g basmati rice
  • Spices (curry etc..)

Préparation

Temps total : 25 min

  • Préparation : 5 min
  • Cuisson : 20 min

Steps

  1. In a cootation, sweat the chopped onions in the olive oil over low heat.
  2. Attention: do not let it color!
  3. Add the rice without washing it
  4. Stir until the grains are almost transparent
  5. Pour the broth and the bay leaf: (the spices if you want)
  6. Bring to a boil, salt. Cover and put in the oven. Cook for 20 minutes.
  7. Egrenner with the fork
