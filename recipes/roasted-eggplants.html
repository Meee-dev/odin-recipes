Tender and tasty roasted eggplants

These delicious roasted eggplants are perfect for accompanying the two previous recipes and all your favorite dishes.

Ingredients for 4 people

  • 4 Eggplants
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • herbes de Provence

Préparation

Temps total : 40 min

  • Préparation : 5 min
  • Repos : -
  • Cuisson : 35 min

Steps

  1. Cut each aubergine lengthwise without peeling them. Band the flesh with a knife.
  2. Spread 1 tablespoon of olive oil over the flesh of each half eggplant.
  3. Add the herbs, salt and pepper.
  4. Bake at 200°C (thermostat 6-7) until the flesh becomes soft. This dish is eaten with a shell and a small spoon.
